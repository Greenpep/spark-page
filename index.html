Privacy Policy

Our Commitment to Privacy

At Spark, we take your privacy very seriously. Your journal entries contain your most personal thoughts, and we are committed to keeping them safe and private.

What Data We Collect

We collect the following information to provide our service:

  • Account Information: Your email address and display name for authentication purposes.
  • Journal Entries: The content you write in your journal entries, which is stored securely and only accessible by you.
  • AI-Generated Insights: The startup ideas and analysis generated from your entries.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to provide and improve the Spark experience:

  • AI Analysis: Your journal entries are processed by AI to identify pain points and generate personalized startup ideas tailored to your experiences.
  • Service Improvement: We may use anonymized, aggregated data to improve our AI analysis capabilities.

We do NOT sell, share, or distribute your personal data or journal content to any third parties for marketing or other purposes.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS.
  • Your journal entries are stored securely in our database with row-level security, ensuring only you can access your data.
  • We use Supabase for our backend infrastructure, which provides enterprise-grade security.

AI Processing

When you use the analysis feature:

  • Your journal entries are sent to AI for processing.
  • The AI analyzes patterns in your writing to identify pain points and generate startup ideas.
  • AI-generated insights are stored in your account and are only accessible by you.
  • We do not use your data to train AI models.

Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as you maintain an active account. If you delete your account, all your personal data, including journal entries and generated insights, will be permanently deleted from our systems.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all your personal data stored in the app.
  • Delete your account and all associated data at any time.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us.
